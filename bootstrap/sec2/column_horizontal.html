justify-content-* Classes These classes control how the columns are horizontally aligned inside the row when there is extra space.

  • justify-content-start → Aligns columns to the left of the row.
  • justify-content-end → Aligns columns to the right of the row.
  • justify-content-center → Aligns columns in the center of the row.
  • justify-content-around → Distributes columns evenly with equal space around them. Equal space around each column (including half-space at the very left and right). The columns look more “spread out” with padding on the sides.
  • justify-content-between → Distributes columns evenly with equal space between them.
  • justify-content-evenly → Distributes columns evenly with equal space around them.Equal space between all columns and on the outer sides. This usually looks the most balanced and evenly distributed.
